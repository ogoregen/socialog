# Plans

## Split media from bookmarks
Books, music, and movies become a separate **media catalogue** with rich metadata, search, and covers. Articles, recipes, places, events, and other links stay as lightweight **Saves**.

## Media search
Replace URL input with real search for books (Open Library), music (Discogs), movies (TMDB). Results show with covers, tap to save — no URL needed.

## Calendar tab
Unified view of events and tasks under date headers. Deferred until media/bookmarks split settles.

## AI recommendations
"For you" section at the bottom of Home. Claude Haiku, ~$0.06/user/month at 1 call/day. Requires backend to keep API key server-side. Not building until backend exists.

## Backend
Needed for AI recommendations, rate limiting, and future sync/social features. App stays zero-backend until then.
